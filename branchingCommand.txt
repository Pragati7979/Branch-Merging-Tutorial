-->git checkout -b branchName

How to come back at master branch?
-->git checkout master

How to merge branch?
-->git merge branchName

How to push branches?
Make changes according to your need. 
Push the branch to the remote 
-->git push origin branchName 
merge the branch
-->git merge branchName
